Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

What age can my child join?

We accept children from age 2 (Nursery) through Grade 5 (age 10–11), with rolling admissions where seats are available.

Can I visit before enrolling?

Yes — we encourage it. Use the form above or call us to book a campus tour at a time that suits you.

Do you offer transport?

Bus routes are available for most neighbourhoods nearby. Ask our front office for the current route map.

What are the fees?

Fees vary by program. Reach out via the contact form and our admissions team will share the current fee structure.
