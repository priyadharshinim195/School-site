Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Little Sparks Nursery & Primary School ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit littlesparks-school.example (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here. This is a template policy — replace the placeholder details (school name, address, contact email) with your real information, and have it reviewed against your local laws before publishing live.

1. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: such as your name, email address, phone number, and message when you use our Contact form to make an enquiry.
  • Automatically collected information: such as your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on the Site, gathered through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Cookies: small text files stored on your device to remember preferences, measure traffic, and — where enabled — support advertising, as described in Section 4.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to admissions enquiries and contact form submissions
  • To improve our Site's content, layout, and performance
  • To understand how visitors use the Site through aggregated analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Children's privacy

This Site is intended for parents, guardians, and prospective families researching our school — it is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 through this Site. Any information about a child (such as an age group mentioned in an enquiry) is provided voluntarily by a parent or guardian through the Contact form. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us directly, please contact us so we can remove it.

4. Cookies and advertising

We may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and, where applicable, to display advertising through third-party providers such as Google AdSense. These providers may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data on partner sites, and manage your ad personalization settings, on Google's own policy pages. You may also disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the Site may not function as intended if you do so.

5. Third-party links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (for example, map services or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites, and we encourage you to review their own privacy policies.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers (such as email or hosting providers) strictly to operate the Site, or where required by law.

7. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@littlesparks-school.example or 123 Learning Lane, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.
