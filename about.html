Our story

Built by teachers, for children who deserve to be delighted

Little Sparks began in 2013 with two classrooms and a simple belief: young children learn best when they feel safe, curious and genuinely happy.

🎯

Our Mission

To give every child a joyful, unhurried foundation — combining play, structure and warmth so learning never feels like a chore.

🌱

Our Vision

A school where children leave every day a little more confident, a little more curious, and excited to come back tomorrow.

What we stand for

Our values

🤝

Kindness first

Every interaction models the empathy we want children to grow up with.

🔍

Curiosity

Questions are welcomed, not shushed. We follow a child's interest wherever it leads.

🧭

Consistency

Predictable routines help young children feel secure enough to take risks and learn.

🗣️

Open communication

Parents get honest, regular updates — never surprises at report-card time.

Milestones

How we've grown

2013

Two classrooms, twelve children

Little Sparks opens its doors with a single nursery batch and a lot of hope.

2017

Primary wing added

We extend our program through Grade 5, so families no longer need to switch schools at age six.

2021

New campus, safety upgrades

A larger campus with dedicated play areas, a sick-bay, and full CCTV coverage.

2026

350+ families and counting

Still small enough to know every child's name — and proud of it.
